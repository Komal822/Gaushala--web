Our Shelter

A True Home for Rescued Gau Mata

Our shelter spans acres of land providing spacious, ventilated, and hygienic living space for over 750 rescued cows. Every cow — whether injured, abandoned, or old — gets a permanent, safe home at Krushnai Goshala Trust with proper bedding, shade, and cleanliness maintained daily.

What Our Shelter Offers

Every corner of our shelter is designed with the comfort and safety of Gau Mata in mind.

Covered Sheds

Strong, roofed sheds protecting cows from rain, sun, and cold throughout the year.

Open Grazing Area

Large open ground where cows can roam, graze, and enjoy natural movement daily.

Secure Boundary

Fenced and gated boundary ensuring no cow can wander onto roads or face danger.

Soft Bedding

Dry paddy straw and comfortable bedding provided for resting and sleeping cows.
