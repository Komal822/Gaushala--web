Daily Nourishment

Nutritious Food & Clean Water Every Day

Feeding 750+ cows daily is a massive responsibility we take with full devotion. Our goshala provides two meals of fresh green fodder, dry feed, and nutritional supplements each day, along with a continuous supply of clean drinking water through dedicated troughs and pipelines throughout the shelter.

What We Provide Daily

A complete nutrition and hydration system keeping every cow healthy and strong.

Green Fodder

Freshly cut green grass and seasonal crops served twice daily for natural nutrition.

Dry Feed & Hay

Dry paddy straw, wheat hay, and dry feed mix provided alongside green fodder.

Clean Water Supply

Dedicated water troughs and pipelines ensuring 24/7 access to fresh drinking water.

Mineral Supplements

Calcium, mineral blocks, and vitamins added to feed for complete cow health.
