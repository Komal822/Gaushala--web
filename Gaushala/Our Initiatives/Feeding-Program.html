Feeding Program

Every day we provide nutritious meals to rescued cows – because a healthy Gau Mata is a happy Gau Mata.

What we feed our cows

Green Fodder

Fresh, organic green grass and maize cultivated on our own land, ensuring high nutrition and digestibility.

Dry Fodder & Concentrates

A balanced mix of dry hay, grains, oil cakes, and mineral supplements to maintain energy and health.

Clean Water & Care

Fresh drinking water available 24/7, and special dietary attention for sick and old cows.
