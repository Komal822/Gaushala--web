Why audits matter

As a registered public trust, we file our audited financials with the Charity Commissioner every year. Our audit reports reflect our commitment to responsible use of donations – every rupee is accounted for and spent on cow welfare.

  • Independent auditors – M/s Sharma & Associates, Chartered Accountants
  • Regular filings – Income tax returns and trust annual reports
  • Open to scrutiny – Donors can request a copy anytime
