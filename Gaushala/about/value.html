Our Core Values

These timeless principles guide every decision, every rescue, and every act of Seva at Krushnai Goshala Trust. They are not just words — they are the foundation of our sanctuary.

🕊️

करुणा

Karuna · Compassion

Every injured, old or abandoned cow receives unconditional love and kindness. We see the divine in every Gau Mata, and respond with a gentle hand and a warm heart — no animal is turned away.

“dayā sarvatra”
🔍

निष्पक्षता

Nishpakshata · Transparency

Our accounts, certificates (80G, 12A, CSR-1) and reports are open for all. Donors and well-wishers can see exactly how every rupee is used for cow protection. Integrity builds trust.

audited annually
🌱

स्थायित्व

Sthāyitva · Sustainability

We grow our own green fodder, harvest rainwater, and use solar power where possible. A self-sufficient goshala ensures that our cows thrive for decades, not just today.

5-acre fodder farm
👥

समुदाय

Samudāya · Community

Gau Seva is a collective effort. We welcome volunteers, celebrate festivals with cows, and involve local families in feeding, grooming, and caring for the herd. Together we serve.

1,200+ volunteers
☸️

अहिंसा

Ahimsa · Non‑violence

All creatures are sacred. Our shelter is a place of complete non‑violence — from the cows to the birds and stray dogs that find refuge here. We promote harmony among all species.

ahimsa paramo dharmaḥ
🔱

भक्ति

Bhakti · Devotion

Serving cows is an act of worship. We begin each day with prayer, gratitude, and the sacred bond between humans and Gau Mata. Spirituality infuses our work with purpose.

daily aarti at 7am
IN ACTION

Values aren’t posters —
they are our daily routine.

From the way we bandage an injured leg to the respect we show each elder cow, our values are visible in every corner of the goshala. Come, experience the difference.
