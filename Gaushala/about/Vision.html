Our purpose

Vision & Mission

Krushnai Goshala Trust, Jamod, exists to protect, serve and honour Gau Mata with unwavering compassion. Our vision is a world where every cow lives with dignity, and our mission turns that vision into action through rescue, shelter, and community involvement.

Est. 2017
Registered Trust
🔭

Our Vision

To create a society where no cow suffers on the streets — every Gau Mata receives unconditional love, proper shelter, nutritious food, and expert medical care. We envision a future where cow protection is a shared responsibility, rooted in Indian tradition and compassion.

Long-term impact: safe shelters across Maharashtra.

⚙️

Our Mission

Rescue stray, injured and abandoned cows; provide lifelong shelter, medical treatment and balanced nutrition. Engage community through Gau Seva volunteering, awareness programs, and transparent CSR partnerships. Sustain the goshala through ethical donations and 80G tax-benefit contributions.

Values that guide us

Compassion

Every cow is treated with kindness, irrespective of age or health.

Transparency

Open books, 80G & 12A certified, clear donor communication.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly shelter, green fodder cultivation, renewable practices.

Community

Involving local volunteers, donors, and families in gau seva.

Our Approach: Service with Dignity

We combine traditional values with modern veterinary care. Each rescued cow receives individual attention, nutritious food, and a clean shelter. We also work with local authorities to prevent roadside accidents and improve animal welfare.
