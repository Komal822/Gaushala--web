A Life Saved – Our Success Story 🐄

A severely injured stray cow was rescued from the roadside and brought to our gaushala. With continuous medical care, nutrition, and love, she fully recovered and now lives safely under our protection.

This is just one of the hundreds of lives saved by Krushnai Goshala Trust through compassion, seva, and dedicated cow protection efforts.
